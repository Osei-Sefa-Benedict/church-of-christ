Finance Ministry

Know More About Our Finance Ministry

  • our Finance Ministry

  • VISION of the Ministry

    Ensuring effective and efficient utilization of and sourcing for finances.

  • MISSION of the Ministry

    To make COC, UG a financially independent church and minimize the extent of sourcing financial aid from neighboring congregations while effectively and efficiently putting the Church money into judicious use [that is, ensuring financial discipline]

  • POLICIES OF THE COMMITTEE

    A.As a source of fund to support the church, consultation of patrons should be considered as well as a strong relationship will be built between the church and its alumni to ensure easy sourcing of funds.

    B. Money will be withdrawn on Sundays to cut down charges from the mobile money system, but all expenses to be incurred by the various committees should be brought to the Finance Committee latest by Friday. Delays will see the transaction not processed. [since we are currently using the Mobile Money System]

    C.In case of Bank Account System, all requests for the week should be made on Sunday after Church Service while withdrawal will be made on either Tuesdays or Wednesdays.

    D. A receipt book will be used to keep record of withdrawals and deposits.

    E.The state of account will be made known to the students at the end of every semester.

    F. All committees should submit their various budgets as early as possible to enhance the quick preparation of the final budget.

  • responsibilities of the Ministry

    1. Keep records of all finances of the Church

    2. Give period accounts of the state of the Church finances

    3. . Withdrawal from account and distribution of money to all Committees

    4. Take care of all expenses during leadership meetings

    5. Other responsibilities that will be assign to the committee by leadership or popup in the course of time.
