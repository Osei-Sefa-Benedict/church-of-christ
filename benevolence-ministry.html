Benevolence Ministry

Know More About Our Benevolence Ministry

  • our Benevolence Ministry

  • MISSION of the Ministry

    To ensure the welfare of the all members of this congregation through supports (in cash and/or in kind) and programs geared towards making all members feel part of the family and also engage in external benevolence that are within the strength of the congregation.

  • policy OF THE COMMITTEE

    A. Benevolence committee, with the help of finance committee, will deal with matters concerning money issues within GHȼ120 without consulting the whole team. After there is a debriefing to the whole leadership.

    B. . Benevolence committee will meet the entire leadership on condition that the amount involved exceeds GHȼ120.

    c. Benevolence committee, in an emergency situation, will respond with the urgency required to the extent the committee can provided it is impossible to contact the finance committee. After, there is a debriefing and refunding.

    D. Occasionally, Benevolence committee will enlighten the church about scholarships and job opportunities available on campus and would advise and help anyone interested in securing these opportunities.

    i. Death of Relative

    A. Basket collection will be made for the transportation of representatives from the church to and from the funeral ceremony. Additions could be made to the amount acquired from the church’s coffers considering the number and distance.

    B. Benevolence committee shall donate to any bereaved member GHȼ150.00. If there is more than one member (siblings) connected to the death, each would receive GHC 100. At the funeral, the benevolence committee would donate GHC 70 to the family.

    C.The church shall donate GHC 150 to the family of a member in sister congregations who dies.

    ii. Marriage

    A. There shall be a basket collection exercise for the transportation of representatives who would attend the ceremony. Possible additions could be made to the amount acquired considering the number and distance.

    B. GHC 150 would be given to a member in support of his or her wedding ceremony. The policy does not discriminate between those who marry within or outside the church. Couples within the church however, receive GHC 150 each.

    c. Benevolence committee will grant GHȼ100.00 to each partner if the couple are members of Church of Christ-UG

    D. Benevolence committee will donate GHȼ100.00 to a member getting married outside Church of Christ.

    E. The whole leadership will decide on the amount to be given to any alumni getting married and has invited the church.

  • responsibilities of the Ministry

    i. Take of all benevolent activities in the Church (the needy, death relative, marriage among other)

    ii. Collaborative Work

    a. Collaborate with Organising Committee in organizing programs such as Fellowship, Gift Day, among other

    iii. Sensitization on scholarship programs and job opportunities on campus and assistance on getting them.

    iv. Benevolence Committee would responsible for collection of clothing from members.

    v. Other responsibilities that will be assign to the committee by leadership or pop-up in the course of time.
