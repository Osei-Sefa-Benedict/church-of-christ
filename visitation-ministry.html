Visitation Ministry

Know More About Our Visitation Ministry

  • our Visitation Ministry

  • Vision of the Ministry

    To see higher attendance and higher participation of members in both attendance and participation in church services and other programs organise by the Church.

  • MISSION of the Ministry

    Is to organise visitation, other programs and collaborate with other committees to ensure higher attendance and participation of members in services and other church programs.

  • OBJECTIVE OF THE COMMITTEE

    A. Increase attendance and improve upon punctuality.

    B. Help or contribute to the spiritual growth of members through working with Evangelism and Edification Committees.

    i. To Evangelism Committee – submit name of individuals (both members and non-members) who need teaching

    ii. To Edification Committee – suggest topics or lessons

    C. Help or contribute to ensuring the welfare of members through working with Benevolence Committee

    D. Ensure unity and boost fellowship among members through organization of visitations and programs (like fellowship, excursions and others)

    E. Help train young members (level 100 and new converts) through assigning of some responsibilities

  • RESPONSIBILITY OF THE COMMITEE

    a. Track attendance and report on that (either to leaders or Church) when the need be and keep records (Membership List, New Converts and Visitors – regular and new)

    b. Appoint Hall Reps and review their reports

    c. Organise and appoint people for visitation, both General and Special

    i. General – Semester’s plan visitation

    ii. Special – Checks (on the sick, absentees and others)

    a) For the sick

     During service days, that is, Sundays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, all members present will go

     Non-service days, that is, Mondays, Tuesdays, Fridays, Saturdays, some members will be selected.

    b) For absentees and others, some members will be selected for the visitation.

    c) Questions to be asked

     State of condition (health, finance, other issues necessary)

     Any Doctrinal issues

     Recommendations, concerns and suggestions to leaders

    d. Preparing of Pairing List and managing the Church’s WhatsApp group

    e. Submit to Evangelism Committee members and non-members who need teachings and to Edification Committee members who can officiate and topic/lesson we think is necessary for the members and also, to Benevolence Committee members who need assistance from the Church.

    f. Welcome visitors after service/worship.

    G. Other responsibilities that will be assign to the committee by leadership or popup in the course of time
