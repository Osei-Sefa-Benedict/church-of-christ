Edification Ministry

Know More About Our Edification Ministry

  • our Edification Ministry

  • Vision of the Ministry

    To equip members for the service of God so that the body of Christ may be built up until all reach the unity of faith and the knowledge of the son of God, and become mature attaining the whole fullness of Christ. Ephesians 4:13.

  • MISSION of the Ministry

    To keep watch over and shepherd the flock of God which He purchased with His blood. Act 20:28.

  • OBJECTIVE OF THE COMMITTEE

    The primary objective of the committee is to divide the membership of the church into groups based on their spiritual needs so that relevant programs can be tailored to satisfy the needs of each group.

  • FUNCTION OF THE COMMITTEE

    A. To ensure that a healthy and conducive environment is provided for spiritually effective worship and fellowship.

    B. Planning and drawing of programs – teachings, sermons, seminars etc. aimed at developing members spiritually and materially.

    C. Oversee the activities of the following ministries of the church:

    i. Women ministry

    D. Periodically assess the spiritual growth of members of the church and provide guidance.

    E. To provide direction on issues relating to doctrine and scripture. 1 Tim. 3:1.

    F. To continually ensure that mature, dedicated and knowledgeable and effective leaders are nurtured for various aspects of church operations like songs leading, prayers, sermons, teaching and etc.

    G. . To train new converts

    H. . Collaborate with evangelism committee to teach new converts and visitors

    i. Other responsibilities that will be assign to the committee by leadership or pop-up in the course of time.

  • ROLES OF THE EDIFICATION COMMITTEE’S HEAD

    The head of the Edification Committee should discharge the succeeding duties:

    i. Supervise all activities of committees.

    ii. Represent the church at official meetings with other bodies such as the UCF.

    iii. Convey all relevant information based on decisions reached to the church.

    iv. Interact with the Staff Village Basic School authorities.

    v. Other responsibilities that will be assign to the committee by leadership or pop-up in the course of time
